How to Capture Screenshots

  1. Open this file in Chrome or Safari
  2. Right-click on each phone mockup and select "Inspect"
  3. In DevTools, click the device toolbar icon
  4. Set dimensions to 1290x2796 (iPhone 14 Pro Max)
  5. Take a screenshot of the entire frame
  6. Repeat for other sizes: 1284x2778 and 1242x2208
